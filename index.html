What I do

I build practical, testable geospatial workflows—turning raw inputs into documented, analysis-ready datasets. I’m comfortable with messy real-world data and I prioritize clear schemas, QA checks, and reproducibility.

  • Ingest + standardize tabular/geospatial sources (ACS, TIGER/Line, local admin data)
  • Transform data (field cleaning, normalization, joins, tract-level aggregation)
  • Validate outputs (schema consistency, missingness checks, geometry QA)
  • Communicate results with tract-level maps and lightweight interactive demos
