What I do

I build practical, testable workflows for geospatial datasets—turning raw inputs into analysis-ready outputs. I’m especially comfortable with messy real-world data and documenting repeatable processes.

  • Ingest and standardize geospatial and tabular datasets (Census/ACS, TIGER/Line)
  • Transform data (clean fields, normalize schemas, spatial joins, aggregation)
  • Validate outputs (geometry checks, QA, schema consistency)
  • Deliver outputs via maps, demos, and clear documentation
