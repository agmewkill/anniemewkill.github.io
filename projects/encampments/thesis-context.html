Encampments & Social Determinants of Health — Thesis Context

This page situates the encampments project within my thesis research on how tract-level social determinants of health (ACS proxies) relate to the spatial distribution of homelessness in San Diego County, using privacy-preserving, aggregated data.

Research framing

Core research question: How do tract-level socioeconomic conditions correlate with the presence and intensity of homeless encampments over time?

  • Unit of analysis: Census tract
  • Outcome: Tract-aggregated encampment counts (PITC)
  • Explanatory variables: ACS 5-year indicators (SDoH proxies)
  • Purpose: Exploratory analysis and model preparation (not causal inference)
