Encampments & SDoH (San Diego) — Thesis context

This page connects the Encampments × ACS workflow to my thesis research on how social determinants of health relate to tract-level homelessness patterns in San Diego County — using privacy-preserving aggregation.

Research framing

Core question: how do tract-level socioeconomic conditions (ACS proxies for SDoH) relate to tract-level encampment presence/volume over time?

  • Unit of analysis: Census tract (no point locations)
  • Exposure/features: ACS 5-year indicators (income, poverty, rent burden, etc.)
  • Outcome: tract-aggregated encampment counts (or rate, if normalized)
  • Output: modeling-ready feature table with documented schema + QA

Privacy + safety note

Encampments are already aggregated by census tract to ensure safety and anonymity. This workflow intentionally avoids publishing point-level locations or revealing sensitive detail.

Data + join logic (high-level)

  • Join key: tract identifier (typically GEOID)
  • Encampments table: GEOID + encampment_count (+ time fields, if used)
  • ACS table: GEOID + selected SDoH indicators (cleaned + standardized)
  • Output: one row per tract (or tract-year), ready for descriptives and models

If you want this to read more “data contract”, add a tiny schema table here (5–8 fields + types).
